Model Data

  • Birthday: October 23 1989
  • Sign: Libra
  • Height: 165cm

Okarie, whose real name is Rie Matsuoka, was born on October 23, 1989, in Chigasaki City, Shonan, Kanagawa Prefecture, Japan. Standing at 165cm, she is known for her stylish appearance and makeup skills. Her blood type is A. Okarie aspires to become a magazine model, hoping to inspire others to enhance themselves after seeing her. She has graced the pages of popular magazines such as Popteen, SCawaii!, Happie nuts, and ViVi.

In July 2010, she became the director of the fashion brand "Ank Rouge." Okarie has also made appearances on TV, such as the show "Mobotama: Girls' Blog" on TV Tokyo in April 2010. Additionally, she has been featured in music videos like SoulJa's "Way to Love The Last Love feat. Miho Karasawa" and S'capade feat. Mao Denda's "Mid Night Sweet."

Okarie's influence extends to the fashion scene, as she served as the cover girl for the first volume of the free paper "TOKYO BLOGGER STYLE." Fans can send their letters to her at the address: 4F, Kanda NK Building, 2-7 Kanda Jimbocho, Chiyoda-ku, Tokyo 101-0051, care of Kawakita Haruki Office.
